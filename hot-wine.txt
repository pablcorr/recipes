Ingredients:
 - 1 orange
 - 1 lime
 - 2 cloves 
 - 2 black tea sac 
 - 2 cinamon stick
 - 80gr sugar
 - 1 red wine bottle  
 - 2-4 stars anise
 - 60 ml water


Directions:
 1. boile the water with the tea, cloves, cinamon, anise
 2. Squeeze the orange and lime
 3. Add the wine
 4. Add the sugar
 5. Reduce the heat and don't let it boile after adding the wine for 10 minutes
 6. Remove the cinamon, tea sacs, cloves and anise
 7. Enjoy!
